# Mode: contact — LinkedIn Outreach

Identify and reach out to key people at target companies.

## Workflow

1. **Identify targets** via WebSearch:
   - Hiring manager of the team
   - Assigned recruiter
   - 2-3 peers (people with similar role)

2. **Select primary target**: The person who would most benefit from you being there

3. **Generate message** using 3-sentence framework:
   - **Sentence 1 (Hook)**: Something specific about their company or current AI challenge (NOT generic)
   - **Sentence 2 (Proof)**: Your biggest quantifiable achievement relevant to THAT role (e.g., "I built an AI agent handling 90% of customer service at my company before selling it")
   - **Sentence 3 (Proposal)**: Quick chat, low pressure ("Would love to chat about [specific topic] for 15 min")

4. **Versions**:
   - EN (default)
   - ES (if Spanish company)

5. **Alternative targets** with justification for why they're good second choices

**Message rules:**
- Max 300 characters (LinkedIn connection request limit)
- NO corporate-speak
- NO "I'm passionate about..."
- Something that makes them want to respond
- NEVER share phone number

---

## Message Template

**Hook + Proof + Proposal**

```
[Specific thing about company/team] + [My relevant achievement] + [Low-pressure ask]

Example:
"Your work on multi-agent systems at Company caught my attention. 
I shipped a similar system handling 85% of customer conversations at my startup (now sold). 
Would love to grab 15 min to discuss orchestration patterns?"
```
