## Fit Assessment

### Why This Works

1. **Systems thinking:** DRAGEN is genomic pipeline automation. You've built pipelines (GPU pipelines in FlipperZillow, data pipelines in PatentIQ).
2. **Signal processing foundation:** HB100 radar (FFT, Kalman filtering, MUSIC algorithms) is adjacent to genomic signal analysis.
3. **Large-scale data:** Mini-Stockfish trained on 316M positions. DRAGEN processes millions of genomic sequences. Scale thinking transfers.
4. **Learning curve:** You learn new domains fast. Bioinformatics is just another domain.
5. **Impact:** Illumina powers healthcare. Your work affects real patients. Meaningful problem space.

### Why It's Borderline

1. **Biotech specialization:** Unlike fintech or ecommerce, genomics is a deep specialized domain. Difficult to exit to AI/web/startup roles later.
2. **Compensation:** £35–45K is significantly below US market. Cambridge is expensive; £40K doesn't go far in UK. You'd earn more at Uber or Adobe by 3–4x.
3. **Location:** Cambridge, England ties you to UK visa/relocation. You can navigate, but less geographic flexibility.
4. **Not your north star:** Your archetype is AI/ML systems. Bioinformatics is cool, but it's genomics infrastructure, not AI.

This is a specialization bet. It can pay off, but it narrows optionality.
