## C. Level & Strategy

**The Core Problem:** This role explicitly requires **7 years of SWE experience + active TS/SCI security clearance**. You're a new grad with ~10 months of professional experience and no clearance. Wyetech is clear in the job post: "United States Citizenship and position appropriate security clearance is required (e.g., Active TS/SCI security clearance with agency appropriate polygraph)."

**Why this is not a path:** Federal contractor roles at this security level cannot hire new grads and sponsor clearances from scratch. Clearances are only granted after extensive background vetting, and the company must already have you on contract. Wyetech listed the clearance as a prerequisite (not "willing to sponsor"), meaning they're looking for candidates who already hold clearances from prior federal roles.

**What would be needed:**
1. 5–6+ years of industry SWE experience first (at non-federal companies)
2. Then transition to a federal contractor with security clearance sponsorship
3. Then apply to roles like this

**Recommendation:** Skip this one. Not a viable path for new grad. The clearance requirement is an automatic disqualifier.
