4. **Generative AI emphasis might be lip service.** The role says "leverage Generative AI and LLMs," but this could mean: using ChatGPT to write copy, or building fine-tuned models. Clarify during interview: "What LLM infrastructure are we building vs. using?"

## Interview Prep Notes

- **Lead with:** PatentIQ (product ownership, end-to-end development, shipping under deadline), Groundwork Books (real-time systems, third-party API integration, financial product context).
- **Domain gap honesty:** They expect you to be learning tax/accounting. Be upfront: "I'm new to fintech, but I learn fast. Can you walk me through how Collective handles IRS deduction rules?" This shows humility + curiosity.
- **Practical coding:** Expect a take-home assignment (usually a small feature: "build a form that validates W-2 income and calculates deductions"). It's straightforward. Focus on clean code + testing.
- **Ask them:** 1. What does "leverage Generative AI" mean in practice? 2. Who's my primary mentor on the team? 3. How does Collective ensure tax compliance (who owns this)? 4. Timeline: when would I be productive on my own?
