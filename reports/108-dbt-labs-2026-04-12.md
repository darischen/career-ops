### Recommended Case Study for Presentation

**Lead with PatentIQ:**
- 4-minute walkthrough: "We built an AI patent search MVP in 4 months for early-stage founders. Here's the architecture."
- Diagram: Frontend (Next.js) → API (FastAPI) → Database (Supabase + pgvector) → LLM (Claude)
- **Key talking points:**
  1. Why pgvector? "We considered MongoDB, Pinecone, and Elasticsearch. pgvector was free, already in PostgreSQL, and good enough for MVP iteration. This was a strategic decision, not technical."

  4. **Why this matters for Fusion:** "Building a query engine requires similar thinking—trade-offs between correctness, performance, and time-to-ship. You can't over-optimize everything."
