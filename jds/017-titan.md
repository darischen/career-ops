# Titan — New Grad Product Engineer

**Location:** San Francisco, California (On-site)

**Employment Type:** Full-time

---

## Company Overview

Titan is an AI holding company specializing in IT services transformation. The organization acquires and partners with Managed Service Providers (MSPs), embedding AI capabilities into their operations. The company has raised over $70M in funding from General Catalyst and acquired market-leading MSPs like RFA.

---

## Role Description

This is not a rotational or shadowing program. New grads will be paired with seasoned senior engineers (many ex-founders) to "ship production code, talk to users, and iterate on AI-native features from day one."

### Key Responsibilities:
- Contribute to production features integrating AI into technician workflows
- Participate in user interviews and feedback sessions
- Own small-to-medium features end-to-end (backend and frontend)
- Balance speed with quality while experiencing rapid company scaling

---

## Requirements

**Essential Qualifications:**
- Built projects outside classwork with real users (SaaS, trading bot, mobile app, etc.)
- Experience handling bug reports and shipping updates

- Proficiency with TypeScript, Python, and React
- Comfortable with self-direction and unblocking obstacles

**Bonus Qualifications:**
- Revenue-generating product experience
- Experience with AI/ML frameworks or LLM API integration
