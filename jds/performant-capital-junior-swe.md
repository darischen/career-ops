# Performant Capital — Junior Software Engineer

## About the job

About Performant Capital

Performant Capital is a Chicago-based private equity firm focused on SaaS and tech-enabled services investing in companies with annual revenue ranging from $3M to $40M. With over 50 years of collective investing and operating experience in these sectors, Performant's principals seek opportunities where its depth of investing, operating expertise, and partner network can materially impact performance.

## Job Summary

Performant Capital is seeking a motivated Junior Software Engineer to support the development of innovative, "skunk works" style projects across our portfolio of companies. This is an entry-level role designed for individuals who are eager to build real-world software, learn quickly, and contribute to high-impact initiatives.

You will work alongside experienced developers and business stakeholders to design, build, and deploy software solutions that create tangible value. This role offers hands-on exposure to full stack development, modern technologies, and direct collaboration with business users.

## Compensation

Base Salary: $85,000 – $95,000 (commensurate with experience)

## Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field (or equivalent practical experience)
- Familiarity with at least one programming language (e.g., Python, JavaScript, C#, or similar)
- Basic understanding of front-end and back-end development concepts
- Exposure to databases (SQL or NoSQL) and version control systems (e.g., Git)
- Interest in learning cloud platforms and modern development tools
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Effective communication skills and willingness to collaborate with both technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Eagerness to learn, adapt, and take on new challenges in a fast-paced environment

## Responsibilities

- Assist in building and maintaining full stack applications from concept to deployment
- Collaborate with senior developers to design and implement scalable solutions
- Work with business users to understand requirements and translate them into technical tasks
- Support integration efforts with existing systems and applications
- Participate in code reviews, testing, and debugging to ensure high-quality deliverables
- Contribute to multiple projects simultaneously while managing priorities effectively
- Continuously learn and apply new technologies, including AI/ML tools and cloud services

## Example Projects

- Assist in building a comprehensive database of specialized healthcare providers
- Support data analysis initiatives involving structured and unstructured datasets
- Help develop conversational tools and automation solutions using modern AI technologies
- Contribute to performance-focused applications, such as interactive data visualization tools
- Research and prototype emerging technologies to support business innovation

## Work Environment

- Based in Chicago, (1 N Wacker Dr Suite 3815) with an expectation to work in-office 4-5 days per week
- Collaborative, fast-paced, and entrepreneurial team culture
- Opportunity for mentorship, growth, and exposure to a wide range of technologies and industries

This role is ideal for someone looking to launch their career in software engineering while gaining hands-on experience building impactful solutions across multiple industries.
