# 8VC — Software Engineer, New Grad (Multiple Opportunities)

**Location:** Various (Austin, TX and others; Seed to Series C stage companies)

**Employment Type:** Full-Time

---

## Company Overview

8VC is a VC firm that connects new grads with early-stage portfolio companies (Seed to Series C). Positions are available at various stages with different tech stacks and focuses.

---

## Example Opportunity: Thorin

**Location:** San Francisco (Seed-stage AI company incubated at 8VC)

**Focus:** AI-powered products with strong technical foundations

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Build user-facing functionality, backend systems, and AI-powered workflows
- Work with senior engineers and founding team
- Meaningful ownership and mentorship from day one
- Work across the stack

---

## Requirements

- Strong generalists early in their careers
- Want to work across the stack, learn quickly, and ship real features
- Used by real customers
- Entrepreneurial mindset
- Passionate about early-stage development
- Seeking swift career growth

---

## How It Works

8VC connects new grads with roles within their portfolio. You can explore opportunities through their job board at jobs.8vc.com or job platforms like Ashby and LinkedIn.
